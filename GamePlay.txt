# THE REVENGE - GAMEPLAY GUIDE

## Game Overview
The Revenge is a first-person shooter game built with Unity and C#. Players must survive by shooting enemies before they get too close. The game features intelligent AI enemies that patrol and chase the player, realistic gun mechanics, and a score-based progression system.

## Objective
- Shoot enemies to score points (5 points per kill)
- Reach 120 points to win the game
- Survive by keeping enemies at a distance
- Game ends if an enemy gets too close

## How to Play

### Controls
- **Mouse Movement** - Look around
- **Left Mouse Button** - Shoot
- **WASD/Arrow Keys** - Move around the terrain

### Gameplay Flow
1. **Spawn** - Player starts in the terrain with a gun
2. **Explore** - Move around to find enemies
3. **Detect** - Enemies appear and patrol the area
4. **Engage** - Shoot enemies when they appear
5. **Score** - Each enemy killed awards 5 points
6. **Win/Lose** - Reach 120 points to win, or lose if enemy touches you

## Game Mechanics

### Enemy AI Behavior
- **Wander State**: Enemies patrol randomly within their designated area
- **Detection**: Enemies detect player within 10-unit radius
- **Chase State**: Once detected, enemies pursue the player using pathfinding
- **Attack**: Game ends immediately when enemy collides with player

### Scoring System
- Points per enemy killed: 5
- Total enemies needed to win: 24
- Win threshold: 120 points
- Score displayed in real-time

### Gun Mechanics
- Raycast-based shooting for accurate hit detection
- Range: 200 units
- Automatic animation and sound effects
- Muzzle flash particle effects
- Instant enemy destruction on hit

## Tips for Success
- Keep moving to avoid being cornered
- Maintain distance from enemies
- Listen for enemy sounds to detect their presence
- Aim carefully - accuracy is key
- Be aware of your surroundings at all times

## Technical Details
- **Engine**: Unity 3D
- **Language**: C#
- **AI**: NavMesh pathfinding system
- **Physics**: Raycast hit detection
- **Target Audience**: Children and youth

## Win/Lose Conditions
- **Win**: Score reaches 120 points (game freezes)
- **Lose**: Enemy collides with player (returns to menu)

This game provides an exciting and challenging FPS experience perfect for quick gaming sessions!
